JONATHAN "JOHN" WICK

THE MAN SENT TO KILL THE BOOGEYMAN

ABOUT

Wick is a legendary hitman who had retired until a gang invades his house, steals his car, and kills the puppy that his late wife Helen had given him. This sets him on a path of revenge, returning to the criminal underworld and eventually coming into conflict with the international organization of assassins of which he was once a part.

GALLERY

2014

Following Helen's death from a terminal illness, Wick largely remains isolated from society. He spends his days driving his prized 1969 Ford Mustang Mach 1, and caring for his new dog, a beagle named Daisy, the last gift from his deceased wife. When Iosef Tarasov, Viggo's son, breaks into his home, assaults Wick, steals his car and kills Daisy, the vengeful Wick goes on a violent rampage. Knowing that Wick will likely stop at nothing to kill Iosef, Viggo attempts to protect his son and sends numerous assassins to stop Wick, but he kills them all. Wick eventually kills both Iosef and Viggo. Afterwards, Wick rescues a pit-bull puppy scheduled to be euthanized from an animal shelter and returns home.

2017

After taking revenge on the Tarasovs, Wick retrieves his prized car from Viggo's brother, Abram, and forms a truce with him. Wick's hopes of returning to retirement are ruined by Camorra crime boss Santino D'Antonio, who demands he complete his blood oath and assassinate Santino's own sister, Gianna D'Antonio, allowing Santino to take her place at The High Table. Wick refuses, and Santino retaliates by destroying his house. Wick reluctantly travels to Rome and fulfills the oath, only to realize that Santino is targeting him for killing Gianna. Santino sends his bodyguard Ares and Gianna's former bodyguard Cassian after Wick, but ultimately Wick kills both. Santino places a $7 million bounty on Wick's head, after which Wick enlists assistance from the Bowery King. Wick, after massacring hordes of assassins, eventually finds Santino in the Continental, where no bloodshed is permitted. Wick breaks the rule by killing Santino on the hotel premises, forcing Winston to declare him "excommunicado", meaning that he has been thrown out of the Continental without any way to call on it again for help. Winston, however, gives Wick one hour to flee before the excommunicado becomes active and the bounty on his head is doubled and offered globally by The High Table.

2019

Excommunicated, exhausted, and wounded, Wick prepares to escape from New York as assassins gather to kill him after learning of the enaction of the $14 million bounty on his head. Fleeing to Morocco using his former allegiances, Wick meets with Sofia, an old friend, manager of the Moroccan Continental, and someone whose marker Wick holds for rescuing Sofia's daughter. Sofia helps him find the Elder, the one man who's above the High Table itself. The Elder agrees to rescind the excommunicado in return for Wick killing Winston and serving the High Table for the remainder of his life. Wick severs his ring finger and offers his wedding ring to the Elder to prove his fealty. Wick then returns to New York and is pursued by assassins until he reaches the safe haven of the Continental. Wick reunites with Winston but decides to spare him. Instead, Wick and the hotel's concierge, Charon, fight against the High Table and their enforcers. Successfully defeating waves of attackers, Winston and High Table representative, The Adjudicator, have a parley on the hotel roof. Ultimately, Winston knowingly shoots Wick in his bulletproof jacket multiple times to restore his standing with the High Table, while not wanting to betray him, and Wick tumbles from the roof into the street. The Bowery King, recovering from grave wounds inflicted by The Adjudicator's assassin named Zero, retrieves the badly injured Wick in his hideout, where he tells Wick that he plans to declare war against the High Table and Wick agrees to join him.
